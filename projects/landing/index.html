Grooming Services

Our Baltimore dog grooming salon offers a safe, clean, calm, comfortable and loving environment to all of the dogs that visit our spa. We use natural, gentle and all chemical-free products containing healing ingredients to bring out the best in your dogs skin and coat. Our groomers take a holistic approach by inspecting the “whole body” (skin/coat, anal glands, ears, teeth/gums and nails).

The process of dog grooming can be very stressful for some pups so we minimize their stay to 3 - 4 hours, and we do not use cage drying methods. We believe that grooming should be a positive experience and each dog is given time to adjust and learn while on the grooming table. Happy Dog is not just a dog grooming salon, but a community of pet lovers who are passionate about caring for all dogs.

Spa Bath

Our basic spa includes a bath with all natural shampoos and conditioners (Oatmeal, Tea tree and Hypoallergenic options available). Blow dry, brush, nail trimming, ear cleaning and gland expression.

Full and Partial Spa

Our full groom includes a bath with all natural shampoos and conditioners (Oatmeal, Tea Tree and Hypoallergenic options available). Blow dry, brush out, customized full body hair cut, nail trim, ear cleaning and gland expression. Partial spa includes a face and sanitary area trim. *Excludes full body hair cut.

Doodle and Poodle Spa

We love our Doodle & Poodle clients and recognize that curly hair takes a lot of care! We’re committed to honestly evaluating each breed and their specific cut and coat needs during each grooming consultation. Please review our doodle and poodle pricing for full and partial groom estimates.
